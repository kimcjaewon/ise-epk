Born in Manila and raised in California, ise (pronounced "ai-ss") is a pop and R&B singer-songwriter now based in Los Angeles, sharing her lived experiences with the world through her sound.

She marked her debut with the single "your loss, baby" under her former name, Denise Rosete, and has since released a string of intimate, hook-driven tracks including "wyd tn?", "Not With You", "we'd be cute." and her 2026 release "Tattoo".

Drawing musical inspiration from Ariana Grande, Jhené Aiko, and Kehlani, ise writes from a place of clarity and vulnerability. Through her music, she hopes to inspire other Asian-American artists to pursue their passions in the creative space.
