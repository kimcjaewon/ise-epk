ise (pronounced "ai-ss") is from Manila by way of California, now living in LA. Right now she tattoos for a living. The long-term plan is to be doing music for a living instead.

Under @tatsbyise she runs a fine-line and traditional tattoo practice with a loyal client base. That's the day job that pays for the dream of doing music full time.

Her 2026 single "Tattoo" takes its imagery straight from the chair: the needle, the permanence, the marks people leave on each other. Earlier releases like "your loss, baby" (released under her old name, Denise Rosete), "wyd tn?", "Not With You", and "we'd be cute." sit in the same emotional territory.

Musically she pulls from Ariana Grande, Jhené Aiko, and Kehlani, and writes pop and R&B for the stuff that's hard to say out loud. She's pushing to make music the main thing one day, and hoping more Asian-American artists feel like they can do the same.
